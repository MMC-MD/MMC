Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our nutrition and wellness programs.

What wellness services does Montgomery Medical Clinic offer?
Our Wellness Center offers nutrition counseling, personalized meal planning, personal training, weight management programs, and holistic health consultations. We combine functional medicine with customized plans for whole-body wellness.
Do I need a referral for nutrition counseling?
No referral is needed. You can schedule a nutrition consultation directly by calling (301) 208-2273 or booking online. Our team will work with you to create a personalized plan based on your health goals.
Does insurance cover nutrition and wellness visits?
Coverage varies by plan. Some insurance plans cover nutrition counseling for medical conditions such as diabetes or obesity. Personal training and general wellness programs are typically self-pay. Call us to check your specific coverage.
Can I combine nutrition counseling with other services at the clinic?
Absolutely. Because our Wellness Center is part of Montgomery Medical Clinic, we can coordinate your nutrition plan with your primary care provider, dermatologist, or other specialists under one roof for a truly integrated approach to your health.
Do you offer personal training at Montgomery Medical Clinic?
Yes! We have a certified personal trainer who designs individualized training programs to improve strength, mobility, and stamina. Training can be paired with nutrition counseling for a comprehensive wellness plan.
