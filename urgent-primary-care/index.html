Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about urgent care and primary care at Montgomery Medical Clinic.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both! We accept walk-ins 7 days a week and also offer scheduled appointments.

Walk-ins are seen on a first-come, first-served basis. For the shortest wait, book same-day appointments online or call (301) 208-2273.

What are your hours? Are you open on weekends?

Yes, we are open 7 days a week, including weekends and most holidays.

Check our website or call (301) 208-2273 for current hours.

What conditions does your urgent care treat?

We treat a wide range of non-emergency conditions, including:

  • Colds, flu, and respiratory infections
  • Minor injuries, sprains, and cuts requiring stitches
  • Allergic reactions, UTIs, ear infections, and rashes

Important: For life-threatening emergencies such as chest pain, stroke symptoms, or severe bleeding, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

Can I establish a primary care doctor at Montgomery Medical Clinic?

Absolutely. We offer ongoing primary care including:

  • Annual physicals and wellness exams
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, asthma)
  • Preventive screenings and immunizations

You can choose a provider and build a long-term relationship with your doctor.

What insurance plans do you accept for urgent care and primary care?

We accept most major insurance plans, including:

  • Aetna, BlueCross BlueShield (CareFirst), Cigna, UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare, Medicaid, Priority Partners, Amerigroup
  • Tricare, and many more

Self-pay options are also available. Visit our insurance page or call to verify your plan.
